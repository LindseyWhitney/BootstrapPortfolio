A bit about me.

I have my Bachelor of Fine Arts Degree in Graphic Design, and have been working professionally as a designer since 2008. A great deal of my experience comes from working for Boise State University. I started there as a designer, moved up to Lead Designer, and eventually became the Creative Director of Student Affairs Marketing. Along the way I won a few awards, and learned a lot. I worked with many great people and groups. But what I missed was the personal relationship that only comes from working one-on-one with a client. So in the summer of 2015, I left my position there to launch Whitney Creative, LLC. It’s been crazy and exhillarating, and I can’t wait to see where it goes from here.
