A Bit About Me.

I have my Bachelor of Fine Arts Degree in Graphic Design, and have been happily creating since 2008. In the summer of 2015 I left my position as Creative Director for Student Affairs Marketing at Boise State University to establish Whitney Creative, LLC. It’s been an exciting journey so far and I can’t wait to see what lies ahead. Other than that, I enjoy traveling, knitting, reading, and plenty of coffee.
