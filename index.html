Hi, I'm Lindsey

I work one-on-one with individuals and small businesses to help them with branding, graphic design, and web design.

A little bit about myself.

I’m a graphic and web designer living in beautiful Boise, Idaho. I received my Bachelor Fine Arts degree in Graphic Design from Boise State University, and if you’ve looked through my portfolio, you’ve noticed I’ve done a lot of work for them over the years. I began working for Boise State professionally in 2008, starting off as a graphic designer, and working my way up to Creative Director in their Student Affairs Marketing Department by 2013. In 2015 I left to pursue a freelance career in design, and brought all the wonderful experience I gained there with me.
