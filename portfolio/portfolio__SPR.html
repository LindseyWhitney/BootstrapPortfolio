Sweet Pepper Ranch

Logo & Brand Development

Sweet Pepper Ranch is a small-scale horse ranch, guest ranch, and horse bed and breakfast located in the Treasure Valley of southwestern Idaho. They take pride in their sustainable practices, and horses are at the heart of all they do. This identity was created to exude their warm, friendly approach to service, as well as pay homage to the “spirit” of the horse—wild and free.

Creative Brief | Overview

Audience:
horse lovers, ranchers, farmers
Tone/Feel:
warm & inviting, modern & clean with a “ranch” feel
Elements:
“spirit” horse (not any specific breed) & sun/sunset
